# InFlux v0.4 Release Notes

## Purpose
Demonstrate that consensus and replay remain reliable under injected faults and partial failures.

## Features Added
- Fault injection harness covering validator dropout, stale validators, corrupted hashes, partitions, late blocks, and invalid states.
- Scenario execution and aggregate resilience reporting.
- Recovery-rate and replay-success tracking under adverse conditions.

## Validation Results
- Fault scenario suite passed with recovery and replay success maintained.
- Divergence was observed and recovered as expected in targeted scenarios.

## Known Limitations
- Cross-platform comparison remained out of scope for this milestone.
- Fault models are scenario-based and do not yet represent full real-world network entropy.

## Next Milestone
v0.5 Cross-Platform Validation: compare environment reports and prove deterministic parity across execution contexts.
